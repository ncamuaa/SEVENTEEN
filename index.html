About SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by PLEDIS Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of 13 members divided into three units: Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance. Known for their synchronized choreography, self-producing abilities, and tight teamwork, SEVENTEEN has gained immense popularity both domestically and internationally.

They debuted with the mini-album "17 Carat" and have since released numerous hits including "Don't Wanna Cry," "Home," "Left & Right," and "Hot." The group's name represents the formula 13 members + 3 units + 1 team = 17.

SEVENTEEN has held successful world tours, topped music charts, and won multiple awards including Bonsang and Daesang awards. Their fandom, known as CARATs, supports them passionately worldwide.
